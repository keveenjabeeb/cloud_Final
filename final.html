The Story About Max Verstappen

Early life

Born in the 30th of September, 1997

It wasn't long before the young Max hit the race track and by 2003 he was karting regularly in Genk, Belgium. In 2005, things got serious and he took part in his first championship, the Belgium Championship Mini (VAS), where he won all 21 races.

Early Stages of Max's F1 Career

He joined Scuderia Toro Rosso in 2015, making him the youngest Formula One driver in history, surpassing the record of Jaime Alguersuari by nearly two years. Max went on to score an impressive 49 points in his debut season, taking two fourth places.
